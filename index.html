Leadership Message

From the Desk of the Principal

Dr. Anil Kumar Sharma

Principal, Pragya Valley Academy

Ph.D. (Education), M.A., B.Ed | 30+ Years Experience

It is with immense pride and gratitude that I welcome you to Pragya Valley Academy — an institution that has been shaping young minds and building futures for over 25 years. Since our founding in 1998, we have held steadfast to our founding principle: that every student deserves access to world-class education, compassionate mentorship, and an environment where they are empowered to discover their true potential.

At Pragya Valley Academy, we believe that education is not merely the transfer of knowledge — it is the transformation of character. Our dedicated faculty does not simply teach subjects; they inspire curiosity, instill values, and walk alongside each student on their journey to excellence. Our consistently outstanding AHSEC results are a testament not just to academic rigour, but to the deep bonds of trust and dedication shared between our teachers and students.

As you explore what Pragya Valley Academy has to offer, I invite you to become part of a legacy that goes beyond classrooms and textbooks. Join us, and together, let us build a future worthy of your dreams.

Dr. Anil Kumar Sharma Principal
Our Legacy

25+ Years of Excellence in Higher Secondary Education

Founded in 1998 by Dr. Ranjan Bora and a team of passionate educators, Pragya Valley Academy started with a simple mission: to provide quality higher secondary education to the students of Assam. What began as a small coaching center with just 50 students has now grown into one of the most prestigious institutes in the region.
