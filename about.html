Our Journey

25+ Years of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1998 by Dr. Ranjan Bora and a team of passionate educators, Pragya Valley Academy started with a simple mission: to provide quality higher secondary education to the students of Assam. What began as a small coaching center with just 50 students in a single classroom has now grown into one of the most prestigious institutes in Northeast India.

Over the past two decades, we have consistently produced top rankers in AHSEC examinations, with many of our alumni going on to become doctors, engineers, civil servants, and business leaders. Our success lies in our unique teaching methodology that combines traditional values with modern educational techniques.

Today, with a campus spanning 5 acres, state-of-the-art facilities, and a team of 50+ expert faculty members, we continue our commitment to nurturing young minds and shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.

Our Philosophy

Academic Approach

The principles that guide our teaching and mentoring

Conceptual Learning

We focus on building strong foundations through conceptual understanding rather than rote learning. Our teachers ensure every concept is understood deeply.

Personal Mentoring

Every student gets individual attention with dedicated mentors tracking their progress, identifying areas of improvement, and providing personalized guidance.

Result-Driven Approach

Our teaching strategies are designed to maximize board exam performance through regular assessments, mock tests, and performance analytics while ensuring deep learning.

Innovation in Teaching

We incorporate modern teaching methods, digital tools, and interactive learning techniques to make education engaging and effective.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we focus on personality development, communication skills, and extracurricular activities to ensure all-round growth.

Values & Ethics

We instill strong moral values, ethics, and social responsibility in our students, preparing them to be good human beings first.
