Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your information

Home / Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

At Pragya Valley Academy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with us.

Our Commitment: We take your privacy seriously and implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1. Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Parent/Guardian information
  • Date of birth
  • Academic records and marksheets
  • Address and location data
  • Photographs and identification documents

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website addresses
  • Cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Admission Processing: To evaluate and process your admission applications
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and send important updates
  • Academic Records: To maintain student records and academic history
  • Improvement: To enhance our website and services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With AHSEC board for academic purposes
  • With government authorities when required by law
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • With your consent or at your direction

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (like Google Forms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

We collect information about students with parental consent. Parents/guardians have the right to review, update, or request deletion of their child's information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with authorities

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the effective date.
